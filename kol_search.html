Read CPE-med, not CPE-mean. Most of these feeds are one viral post sitting on a low baseline, so the average is a flattering lie. The "spiky" tag flags it.

The mean makes bad creators look cheap. 栗子聊跨境 looks like ¥4/like on the mean, but its median post gets 72 likes, so the honest cost is ¥278/like (one 45k post carries the average). Same trap for lele, Cling柯林, Rachel, JoJo, 英国王大拿.

Best value AND on-message (high content, honest-low CPE, consistent): 腾哥说跨境 (content 88, CPE-med ¥6, steady), COCO在硅谷 (85, ¥9), 硅谷鸣姐 (71, ¥7), 超级小恒 (87, ¥17, the most consistent operator). These are where spend works hardest.

The on-bet bullseye operators are expensive per typical post (lele ¥66, Cling ¥49, Rachel ¥49, JoJo ¥188 on the median). You pay for content fit and authenticity, not for organic reach — which is exactly why paid amplification, not their feed, carries distribution.
