Content plan: what the 16 creators actually make

The brief layer on top of the KOL search list. One message spine, three content lanes, the demo account as the production backbone, and a full Chinese production brief per creator, ready for the agency. Picks can still change on the search list; swapped-in creators get briefs the same way.

Built 9 Jun 2026 · briefs cover the 16 pre-checked defaults: 11 video (¥52,667) + 5 图文 (¥24,166) = ¥76,833 of ¥144,000

01The job

Every piece introduces 万里汇 (WorldFirst) to a cold overseas-Chinese viewer who sources from China and sells in the US or UK. One walk-away fact: this is the account you use to pay 1688 and suppliers from overseas, same day for about nine in ten payments, with transparent FX, and to collect your US dollar sales. One action: search 万里汇 WorldFirst. Everything else (sourcing tips, founder stories, FX math) is the wrapper. Organic reach is a bonus: every piece gets boosted, so the brief optimizes for message clarity and trust, not the algorithm.

02Message spine

Must land (pick 3 per piece)

  • Pay 1688 / Chinese suppliers directly from overseas (跨境宝)
  • About nine in ten payments arrive same day
  • Collect Amazon / Walmart / Shopify / TikTok Shop payouts in the same account
  • FX at market rate plus a small visible margin
  • CTA: search 万里汇 WorldFirst

Never say

  • 不要说「用支付宝给供应商付款」，万里汇没有这个功能。
  • 不要承诺汇率、不要说「零费用」「最便宜」。
  • 不要把演示账户的数字说成自己的真实收入。
  • 专业词第一次出现要用大白话解释（比如：换汇 = 把美元换成人民币）。

04The demo account (production backbone)

Every creator shows the real product on screen. Video creators screen-record it; 图文 creators screenshot and annotate it. Proof on screen, not borrowed face-trust.

Per-creator demos: each booked creator gets their own demo account (unique gated link + password, their company name and plausible business numbers in the interface), generated per influencer via the /wf_demo pipeline once the batch locks. The shared benchmark below is for brief-writing and familiarisation.

  • 每位博主会拿到一个专属的官方演示账户（独立链接 + 密码），界面里是为你定制的公司抬头和业务数据，录屏、截图都用它。
  • 通用参考版（拍摄前先熟悉界面）：https://socialworldfirst.github.io/wf-portal-demo/?skiplogin=1
  • 录屏请全屏录制页面内容，裁掉浏览器地址栏。
  • 演示数据仅用于展示流程：可以演示操作，不可以说这些数字是自己的真实流水。
  • 可演示页面：首页余额总览、跨境宝（1688付款）、汇兑中心（换汇计算）、World Card。

05Boost-ready specs

Video

竖屏 9:16，45-90 秒，前 3 秒出钩子，全程字幕，产品演示录屏占全片 20-40%，结尾不留长尾静帧（素材要投流）。

图文

6-9 张图 + 封面。封面大字在缩略图能读清；图内步骤要编号；最后一张是行动卡（搜索 万里汇 WorldFirst）；正文带相关话题标签。

06Workflow, waves, compliance

Flow: briefs out via the agency → creator draft in 7-10 days → ONE WorldFirst review pass (claims and compliance only, never style) → publish in waves → boost behind each wave after 24-48h of organic read.

Compliance: every note registered as brand cooperation via 蒲公英 (RedNote's official brand-collab platform); unregistered brand content cannot be legally boosted and risks reach limits. Usage rights for boosting (聚光 / 薯条) written into every booking.

Wave 1马克Malik · lele · Tina + 龚希望 (图文). Wave 1 sets the standard: the three most native operators plus the strongest saver.
Wave 2Livra · Viola · 圣总 + Chestnut · 克克 (图文). Wave 2 widens the operator story and lands the first build-in-public posts.
Wave 3COCO · 一只树懒 · JoJo + 黄绿 (图文). Wave 3 takes the beginner and side-hustle audiences.
Wave 4Rachel · 索亚 + 课代表 (图文). Wave 4 closes with safety and finance angles.

07Measurement, and the one open decision

Video: boosted view-through, profile visits, comment quality (are people asking how to open the account).

图文: saves first, then on-platform search ranking for payment-intent queries (海外 1688 付款 type searches).

Open decision for the team before the first post: signup attribution. RedNote gives weak click-through, so decide the CTA mechanics now: a per-creator signup keyword, a "where did you hear about us" survey field, or bio links where allowed. Until then the CTA stays search 万里汇 WorldFirst.
